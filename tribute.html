Biography

Manny Pacquiao is a Filipino professional boxer, media celebrity, and politician who became world-famous for winning boxing titles in more weight classes than any other boxer in history. He rose from abject poverty to the pinnacle of his sport, and he also eventually became a senator in the Philippines.


Manny Pacquiao, in full Emmanuel Dapidran Pacquiao, byname Pac-Man, (born December 17, 1978, Kibawe, Bukidnon province, Mindanao, Philippines), professional boxer, media celebrity, and politician who became world-famous for winning boxing titles in more weight classes than any other boxer in history. His rise from abject poverty to the pinnacle of his sport was made even more remarkable by his life outside the ring. The charismatic “Pac-Man” was an idol and a unifying force in the Philippines, where his unprecedented popularity led to commercial endorsements, movies, television shows, CDs, and his image on a postage stamp.


While Pacquiao thrive for his boxing career, he also serves as a Senator in his home country the Philippines in the year 2016-2022. Earlier this year Pacquiao also tried his luck by running for the Presidency, but unfortunately he did not won.

Author's Appreciation

When I was a kid, I always wondered what was so special about boxing that every house in our neighborhood would watch every fight of an underdog Filipino fighter. It turns out, it's not about boxing anymore, it's about a fighter proudly representing his own country behind his back against the world. That is when I learned about our "Pambansang Kamao" Manny Pacquiao. Upon learning Manny's backstory, I learned that he also worked his way from the bottom to the peak of his career. He experienced what it is to have nothing, and the struggles, as well as the hardships that we need to endure to achieve the things we want in life.


Manny's boxing career was extraordinary, he is the only boxer who has 12 belts in 8 different weight divisions. Manny experts also argue that Manny is in the Top 5 boxers of all time. Apart from being one of the best boxers, Manny remained humble and helpful to his fellow countryman. This is one of the things that I love about Manny, he does not hesitate to help those people in need. He always give back to the Filipinos who supported him from the start. Manny is also a religious person, in fact, he is a world-class youth pastor. Not only that, but Manny also served as a Senator in the Philippines, he authored and co-authored many laws that benefit the Filipinos. With the things Manny achieved in his life, I must say the list will never end, this just shows how impactful and inspirational Manny is.


With all the things Manny achieved in life, he remained humble and a good person, Hence, I decided to present this tribute page to the person who inspired me to work hard in life, to not forget to help people along the way and to trust in God's perfect plan.
